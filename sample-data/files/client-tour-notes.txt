Client tour notes - last-mile shortlist

18 Beacon Freight felt strongest in person: two dock doors, proper truck court depth, trailer parking, and a clean path to Main West customers. The space is 36,000 SF, asking $26/SF, and available immediately.

42 Spruce Flex is smaller at 26,000 SF and asks $29.50/SF. It has a grade-level door and a small shared yard. Ceiling height is only 20 ft, but it is under budget and available in July.

510 River Cold Storage has real cold-storage potential and eight dock doors, but the September availability makes it weaker for a near-term logistics tenant.
